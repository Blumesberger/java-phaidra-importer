University of Vienna - University of Padua

Stands for: Permanent hosting, archiving and indexing of digital resources and assets.
Is a management system of digital objects with functions of long-term storage.


  • Easy: The description and indexing of digital objects are simple and fast. The excitability of each uploaded asset is guaranteed by a permanent link.


  • Reiable: The application is based on software Fedora Commons Repository. The format adopted for metadata and digital objects guarantee the long-term archiving and interoperability with other systems.


  • Safe: Thanks to the definitions of the conditions d 'use and a licensing system for objects loaded.
