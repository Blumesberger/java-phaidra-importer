University of Vienna - University of Padova

Hence, the university of Vienna Phaidra project was selected, and a profitable collaboration with the University of Padova started in 2010.

  • Easy to use: Describing and indexing digital objects is simple and fast. Each resource in the database can be easily cited thanks to a permanent link.
  • Reliable: The platform is based on the Fedora Commons Repository software. The formats adopted for the metadata and the digital object garatee long-term data archiving and interoperability with other systems.
  • Secure: Security is guardanteed both by the well-defined terms of use and the licensing system that regulates the upload of the digital objects.
