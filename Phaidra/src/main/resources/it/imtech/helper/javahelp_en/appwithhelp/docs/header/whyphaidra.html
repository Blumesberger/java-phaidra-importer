Why Phaidra

In the library system of the University, following the implementation of several digitization projects it has revealed the need to identify a unique system for:

  • Manage the service of long-term preservation of digital assets

  • Enhance the intellectual production of the university, past and present, in an environment free from commercial suppliers.

  • Managing large collections of media objects with a controlled system of authorizations.



The choice fell on the project PHAIDRA University of Vienna, with which began a fruitful collaboration from 2010.
