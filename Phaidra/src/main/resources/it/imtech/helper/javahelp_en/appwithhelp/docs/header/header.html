Permanent Hosting, Archiving and Indexing of Digital Resources and Assets!

Describing and indexing digital object is simple and fast!

  • Description
    Phaidra is the acronym for Permanent hosting, archiving and indexing of digital resources and assets.
  • Access to Phaidra
    Everyone has open access to the searching and browsing of the archived digital objects.
  • Why Phaidra
    Following various digitisation projects carried out by the university of Padova Library System, a need to find a comprehensive system emerged.

Phaidra is partner in many European projects such as Linked Heritage, Tempus, OpenAire
